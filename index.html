Privacy

Stickr+ collects no data. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no network requests. Every sticker you import stays in the app storage on your device and is never uploaded anywhere.

Common questions

How do I use my stickers in Messages?

Open any conversation, tap the + next to the text field, and choose Stickr+. Tap a sticker to send it, or drag it onto an existing message bubble to stick it there.

How do I add my own stickers?

In the Library tab, tap the + button and pick From Photos or From Files. Still images and animated GIFs both work. Each sticker is automatically placed on a standard canvas so everything sends at a consistent size.

My sticker will not import

Animated GIFs must be under 500 KB, which is an iMessage limit. Try a shorter loop, fewer frames, or smaller dimensions. Still images are resized automatically and rarely hit the limit.

What are packs?

Packs are groups you create for any theme you like. Stickers can belong to more than one pack, and deleting a pack never deletes the stickers inside it. They stay in your library.

I deleted a bundled sticker by mistake

Go to Settings, then Restore Bundled Stickers, to bring the built-in set back.

Do my stickers sync between devices?

Not currently. Because nothing leaves your device, imported stickers live only on the device you added them to.
