Privacy Policy

Stickr+ · Last updated 21 July 2026

The short version

Stickr+ does not collect any data about you. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising identifiers, and no third-party SDKs. The app makes no network requests of any kind.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect, transmit, store on our servers, or share any personal information, usage data, or device identifiers. We operate no servers that the app communicates with.

Your stickers

Stickers you import from Photos or Files are copied into a private storage container that belongs to Stickr+ on your own device. This container is shared only between the Stickr+ app and its Messages extension so that your library appears in both. Your stickers are never uploaded, backed up to us, or made visible to anyone else.

Your packs, favourites, and appearance settings are stored the same way, on device only.

Deleting the app removes this container and everything in it.

Photos and Files access

When you import a sticker, iOS shows its own picker. Stickr+ receives only the specific images you choose. The app does not request access to your full photo library and cannot browse it.

Stickers you send

When you send a sticker in Messages, it is delivered by Apple Messages just like any other attachment. That transmission is handled entirely by Apple and is covered by Apple privacy policy. Stickr+ has no visibility into your conversations.

Children

Stickr+ is rated 4+ and collects no data from users of any age.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date at the top.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue and we will respond there.
